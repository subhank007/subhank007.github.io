Hi my name is Subhan, I am going to start learning pentesting in the field of cybersecurity from scratch. Right as of now I only know what a pentester means:
  someone who is hired to legally break into websites, networks, and apps. Afterwards they explain how they did it and a solution to fix it. 

I am typing this blog to document everything as I learn it. Mostly so I remeber it, also so that a year or more from now theres a public record of the work I put in. 

Here's my rough plan:
- Learn the basics: how computers, networks, and the web actually work
- Learn Linux and the command line
- Learn to find and exploit web vulnerabilities
- Earn hands-on certifications and build a portfolio of writeups
- Practice on legal platforms and, eventually, bug bounty programs

First stop: the TryHackMe Pre Security path. I'll write up what I learn as
I go. If you're starting out too, follow along.
